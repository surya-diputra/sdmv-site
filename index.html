Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

At SDMV, we are fully committed to protecting your security. This Privacy Policy details the processes, permissions, and data safety practices of the SDMV mobile application.

1. Core App Permissions

Because SDMV acts as a local telemetry gauge, specific permissions are required strictly to perform on-device updates. None of this data is collected, stored, or sent to remote servers:

  • Location Information (GPS): Necessary to calculate vehicle speeds, duration logs, and drag timer metrics.
  • Foreground Services: Used to maintain calculations if the phone is placed in a pocket during active riding.

2. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

This app displays ads managed by Google AdMob. AdMob uses unique advertising identifiers (such as the device's Google Advertising ID) to deliver relevant promotions. No identifiable personal profiles are compiled.

3. Information Storage and Deletion

Because SDMV does not use remote databases to store your riding records, all configurations and data logs can be instantly wiped by selecting "Clear Data" under Android operating system settings, or by simply uninstalling the app.

For questions regarding the application, please contact support directly at: support@sdmv.site.
